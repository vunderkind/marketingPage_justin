    Dead or Alive?

    Introducing the world's trickiest drinking game

    Challenge everything you think you know.
    Play Celebrity Dead or Alive to see how much you really know
    about which dear celebrity is still with us or gone to the Great Beyond.

    What's this game?

    Designed to be the ultimate combination of speed and brains, Celebrity Dead or Alive challenges you and your friends' decades' worth of TV trivia to see just how you've kept up with the lives of celebrities beyond the silver screen.
